Words

Inspirations

My memory fades away like flowing sand. Every granule is a transient fracture of life.

All of them deposited in the desert of oblivion, awaiting to be picked up... and then cached again.

It only takes a few minutes to die, but it takes a life to live.

Living is a feat by itself.

I continually consumed myself in this shell. The reality is like a long dream.

Yet sometime I was truly living, and henceforth, I could say, I am my own hero.

In the rest of the time, I was just a parasite of the world.

We often squeeze the world into a thread and see as one-dimensional creatures:

There are always someone behind, and someone ahead.
